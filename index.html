ABOUT

A 14-hour hackathon for high-school students taking place on April 26, 2025 online!

WHO?

Open to all high-school students grades 9-12, and to all experience levels! Whether you're a beginner or advanced, you're invited to MountainHacks 2025.

WHAT?

An online hackathon with tons of cool stuff: workshops, prizes, mini events, and dev tools for ALL hackers!

WHY?

Whether you're looking to learn new coding skills or build revolutionary apps and win prizes, MountainHacks 2025 is for YOU! We'll have developer tools, opportunities, and workshops for everyone.

WHEN?

MountainHacks 2025 will be taking place on April 26, 2025 the whole day. Get ready for 14 hours of hacking!
